Haiku selections

Winter

宿かせと
刀投げ出す
吹雪かな

"Yado kase!" to
Katana nage-dasu
Fubuki kana!
– Buson

"Give lodging tonight,"
He shouts, flinging down his sword.
See the windblown snow!

Spring

梅が香に
のっと日の出る
山路かな
– Basho

Ume-ga-ka ni
Notto hi no deru
Yamaji kana.

On sweet plum blossoms
The sun rises suddenly.
Look, a mountain path!

Summer

夏痩せと
答えてあとは
涙哉
– Kitamura Kigin

Natsu-yase to
Kotaete ato wa
Namida kana

Oh, I answered, my thinness is caused by the summer heat, and burst into tears


Autumn

ほととぎす
大竹藪を
もる月夜

Hototogisu
Ootakeyabu wo
Moru tsukiyo

Hear the sweet cuckoo.
Through the big bamboo thicket
The full moon filters.
– Basho
